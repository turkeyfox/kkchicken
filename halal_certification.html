Halal Certification

At Krispy Krunchy Chicken we hold ourselves and our food to the highest standards. One of those standards is Halal. Halal means "permissible" in Arabic and details the guidelines for food fit for consumption for Muslims, similar to the Jewish concept of Kosher. For the convenience of any of our customers who might happen to be Muslim, all of our menu items are certified halal. Our poultry is certified by ISWA.

To Whom It May Concern

We certify that the poultry slaughtered by Tyson Foods P-1315 does not use rapic decapitation process and have been slaughtered and processed according to Islamic Dietary & Sharia laws under the auspices and supervision of a trained Muslim officer of ISWA for Halal compliance.

The Islamic Society of Washington Area (ISWA) has halal guidelines and procedures national and internationally recognized. The center will issue Halal certificates for the poultry in accordance with the generally accepted and approved halal standards around the world. These services consist of periodic inspection audits for halal compliance by our assigned representatives to ensure quality assurances and to protect the safety and religious rites of Muslim consumers.

With best regards

Hajj Habib Ghanim Senior
President, ISWA Halal Certification Department
